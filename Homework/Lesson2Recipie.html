EASY LEMON CAKE (ALL-IN-ONE LEMON SPONGE)

Easy Lemon Cake. A delicious, moist all-in-one lemon sponge. Perfect for a teatime treat, cake sale or as a birthday or celebration cake.

Ingredients

  • 225 g self-raising flour
  • Sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 225 g soft margarine or butter
  • 225 g caster sugar
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 2 tsp milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 160C/140C fan. Line your baking tins (I use 2 x 20cm round sandwich tins) with baking parchment or reusable baking liners.
  2. Finely grate the zest of two lemons.
  3. Sift the self-raising flour (225g) and baking powder (2 tsp) into a large bowl.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients (4 medium eggs, 225g soft margarine or butter, 225g caster sugar, grated lemon zest and 2 tsp milk) and beat (with an electric mixer on a slow speed or by hand) until combined. Make sure that there are no small lumps of butter/margarine in your mixture. If there are, beat for a little longer until the mixture is smooth.
  5. Split the mixture equally between the pre-prepared tins. Use the back of a spoon to spread the mixture evenly in the tins.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes. To check if the cake is cooked insert a skewer into the centre and if it comes out clean the cake is ready.
  7. Leave the cake to cool in the tins for about 10 minutes. Remove them from the tins and then move them to a wire rack to cool completely. Make sure the cake has cooled completely before decorating.
